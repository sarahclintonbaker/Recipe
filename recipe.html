Pavlova Torte Royale

Serves 8

Please check the ingredients you have already:

    Ingredients - Shell

  • 2 Egg Whites
  • 1/2 Tsp Vinegar
  • Salt
  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 1/2 Tsp Cinnamon

    • Ingredients - Topping

  • 6 oz. Semi-Sweet Chocolate
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 1/4 Cup Water
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 1/4 Cup Sugar
  • 1/4 Tsp Cinnamon
  • Chopped Pecans/Strawberries (optional)

Instructions

Psst! You can mark down steps as you go

For Shell:

Preheat oven to 275 degrees.

Add salt and cinnamon to egg whites and beat until peaks form.

Gradually add sugar and vinegar, beating well after each addition, until meringue is stiff.

Spread on brown paper in an 8" circle and bake for 1 hour.

Turn oven off, let cool in oven for 2 hours.

For Topping:

Melt chocolate with water.

Spread 2 Tbsps over bottom of shell.

To remaining chocolate add beaten yolks, blend well and chill until thick.

Whip cream, sugar and cinnamon.

Fold gently into chocolate to create marbled effect.

Spread over chocolate in the shell and chill.

Garnish with whipped cream and chopped pecans/sliced strawberries.

Let them eat cake!
